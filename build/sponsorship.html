Newport Rocketry has long cultivated an environment of fun and gentle learning. The club aims to be inviting but focused on rocketry, and our passion for it is clearly reflected in our achievements. Although we have some opportunities to gain money throughout the year, our club is run mostly on its own internally supplied funds. As a result, sponsorships are always welcome!
